Frequently Asked Questions About the Ten Commandments

These quick answers summarize the SDA biblical approach and help searchers find direct clarity fast.

Are Christians still expected to keep the Ten Commandments?

Yes. In this framework, salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus, and obedience is the fruit of that relationship—not the price of salvation.

Why does the Sabbath matter in Seventh-day Adventist teaching?

The seventh-day Sabbath is understood as a creation memorial, a sign of worship loyalty, and a weekly rhythm of rest in God.

Where should a beginner start?

Start with the main study, then the mini-series, then devotionals for daily application and prayer-centered obedience.
