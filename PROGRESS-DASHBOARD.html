Discovered Issues

59 Broken Share Links
Share buttons across 9 files still point to .com instead of .web.app. These 404 when shared on social media.
SVG Share Image
The OG share image is SVG format. Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp cannot render SVG previews. Needs a 1200×630 PNG or JPG.
Triple Firebase SDK (~600 KB wasted)
Three different Firebase SDK versions loaded simultaneously (v9.22.2, v10.12.0, v10.12.2), adding ~600 KB of redundant JavaScript.
6 Hotlinked Pinterest Images
6 images are served directly from Pinterest's CDN. These can break at any time. All should be downloaded and hosted locally.
8 Google Font Families (~500 KB)
Importing 8 different font families adds ~500 KB of render-blocking CSS. Reducing to 3 core families would significantly improve LCP.
Analytics Configured But Silent
Measurement ID G-LXVCQNJ6VJ exists in the config, but gtag.js is never loaded. Zero data is being collected.

Copilot Suggestions

01
Create a "Start Here" Page
New visitors don't know where to begin. A guided entry page that routes users to Studies, Devotions, Games, or Videos based on their interest would reduce bounce rates and improve engagement.
02
Add "Reading Time" Estimates
Display estimated reading time (e.g., "5 min read") on studies and devotions. This sets expectations, builds trust, and can improve click-through rates from search results.
03
Create Topical Landing Pages
Build topic cluster pages (e.g., "Prophecy", "Salvation", "Prayer") that link out to all related content. These hub pages signal topical authority to Google and attract long-tail searches.
04
Add "Share Your Testimony" Feature
User-generated testimonies create fresh content, build community, and can dramatically increase return visits. Consider a simple form powered by Firebase Firestore.
05
Launch Weekly Devotional Email
A weekly devotional email newsletter keeps your audience engaged, drives repeat traffic, and builds a direct relationship that isn't dependent on Google's algorithm.
06
Target "People Also Ask" Questions
Research PAA questions related to your topics (e.g., "What does the Bible say about angels?") and write structured Q&A sections to capture Google featured snippets and PAA boxes.
