# Track C — Episode 1 Script
## Title
**God’s Law Is Love, Not Control**

## Format
- Duration: 60–90 seconds
- Audience: Youth / young adults
- Tone: Clear, compassionate, confident

## Script
**[Hook | 0:00–0:10]**
What if the Ten Commandments were never meant to chain you… but to protect your freedom?

**[Core | 0:10–0:40]**
A lot of people hear “commandments” and think pressure.
But Scripture reveals something deeper:
God’s law is love in action.
It teaches us how to live without destroying ourselves or hurting others.
When God says “do not steal,” “do not lie,” “do not kill,”
He is not taking life from you —
He is guarding your future, your peace, your relationships.

**[Bridge | 0:40–0:55]**
Jesus didn’t remove that love.
He fulfilled it and writes it in our hearts.
Obedience is not earning salvation.
It is the fruit of loving the Savior who already gave everything for us.

**[Call to Action | 0:55–1:10]**
This week, choose one commandment and live it intentionally.
Not out of fear — out of love.
And watch how your spiritual life gets clearer, stronger, and more peaceful.
