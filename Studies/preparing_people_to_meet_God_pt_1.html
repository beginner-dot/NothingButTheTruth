Preparing People to Meet God

Part 1

May The Lord our God Bless You for the time you are spending on his word. It is of upmost importance to read The Word of God because it is the Manifestation of His Character to us, His beloved children. Each day should begin with prayer and a reading of The Word [even if it’s just a single Bible verse]. Reading The Word should always be accompanied by prayer, this invites the Holy Spirit Himself together with His ministering angels to come and explain the word to you, to provide you with a clear understanding and spiritual growth.

As much as we need to read about Him and learn more about Him, someone else out there needs to hear about Him, even more than you need to because people out there do not really know about God. They think they know, but great agencies that are against God have tampered with the truth, they are trying by all means to erase The Lord Jesus Christ out of the picture, replacing him with many gods, gods who; according to them, act as mediators between humanity and God. These agencies have given the position of Jesus Christ to other deities.

Jesus Christ is the Son of The Most High. He Was with God, and He was God in the very beginning of Earth’s creation. John 1 :
1 In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God. 2 The same was in the beginning with God. 3 All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing made that was made. 14 And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us, (and we beheld his glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father,) full of grace and truth.

He is coming back to claim Planet Earth as His Kingdom soon, He is coming to destroy sin forever. The sad part is the fact that many, many people are going to be destroyed in this process. So Before He comes, It is His desire for us to have aligned our characters with His Character, because that is the only thing That is going to save us from God’s future wrath.

2Thessalonians 1 :
    7 And to you who are troubled rest with us, when the Lord Jesus shall be revealed from heaven with his mighty angels, 8 In flaming fire taking vengeance on them that know not God, and that obey not the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ:

We have a duty to prepare people to meet The True Mediator and The Real Advocate of humanity. Jesus Christ is the Living Word of God, we have to share His word with the world, urging them to prepare their Characters for The New Jerusalem because God’s Holy city is reserved for those who Know and Love God.

John 14 :
         1 Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God, believe also in me.     2 In my Father’s house are many mansions: if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you.     3 And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also.     4 And whither I go ye know, and the way ye know.          15 If ye love me, keep my commandments.     16 And I will pray the Father, and he shall give you another Comforter, that he may abide with you for ever;     17 Even the Spirit of truth; whom the world cannot receive, because it seeth him not, neither knoweth him: but ye know him; for he dwelleth with you, and shall be in you.

We don’t need too much knowledge to share His Word, we just need to have a personal, individual relationship with Him. When we stay in constant communication with Him [Pray without ceasing _ 1Thessalonians 5:17] we will be cooperating with him and His angels will work in our favor:

Christ’s Object Lessons page 341:
    ”His angels are appointed to watch over us, and if we put ourselves under their guardianship, then in every time of danger they will be at our right hand. When unconsciously we are in danger of exerting a wrong influence, the angels will be by our side, prompting us to a better course, choosing our words for us, and influencing our actions”

If you strive to have His Character, your life will be a living sermon to other people, and they will have a desire to draw closer to God just by observing how you live, without you even saying anything because God’s Character is The most powerful Character that has ever existed, and that exists.
