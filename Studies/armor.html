Armor of God: Gear Up for Spiritual Battle

Ephesians 6 Unpacked — Practical, Powerful, and Needed

Stand Firm in the Full Armor of God

The Apostle Paul commands us in Ephesians 6:10-18 to "put on the whole armor of God" to stand against the devil’s schemes. This armor isn’t just metaphorical — it’s spiritual gear designed to protect and empower you in everyday battles.

Belt of Truth

“Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth...” (Ephesians 6:14)

Truth is your foundation. Lies from the enemy try to destabilize you, but God's truth keeps you steady and strong.

Breastplate of Righteousness

“...and having on the breastplate of righteousness...” (Ephesians 6:14)

This guards your heart and conscience, protecting you from guilt and condemnation by Christ’s righteousness.

Shoes of the Gospel of Peace

“And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace.” (Ephesians 6:15)

Be ready to stand firm and move forward by sharing the peace and hope of Jesus.

Shield of Faith

“Above all, taking the shield of faith...” (Ephesians 6:16)

Faith deflects the fiery darts of doubt, fear, and temptation.

Helmet of Salvation & Sword of the Spirit

“And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.” (Ephesians 6:17)

The helmet protects your mind with the hope of salvation. The sword, God’s Word, is your offensive weapon against lies and attacks.

“Put on the full armor of God, so that you can take your stand against the devil’s schemes.” — Ephesians 6:11

Challenge

  • Identify which piece of armor you feel weakest in today.
  • Pray for God to strengthen that area in your life.
  • Memorize Ephesians 6:11 as your battle cry.
