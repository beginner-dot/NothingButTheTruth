God has a purpose each time He communicates

A Short study on how God's message always reaches us in time.

There is a reason why You are reading this. Do you know why?

That is because you are Going to learn or [if you already know] remind yourself about the importance of God’s communication. See that’s how God works, when you pray in faith before studying The Word, The Holy Spirit draws near, and ministering angels of God come to help you in your study. Then; With The Spirit of God and His living intelligences surrounding you, You will be directed [ultimately if not initially] to the correct message you are supposed to be reading for that specific moment. You will also receive the correct understanding of the subject - I have experienced this for myself countless times.

1 Timothy 3 : ”14 But continue thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them; 16 All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness: 17 That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.”

God always ensures that you receive the message that you need at the time that you need it. It does not mean that He is going to reveal the future to us like he did to the prophets, it means that when The Great Teacher teaches you a lesson, He ensures that you receive the lesson at the relevant time and for the correct reason. Because of his Omnipotence and Almighty Wisdom, He has all the power to know and create anything and everything - He is the one who created and controls the past, the present and the future therefore He knows when exactly you need to learn each lesson, and why.

Sometimes it might feel like You are learning about something when it’s already late for the lesson, but know that a lot of times, God uses His infinite wisdom and love in ways we cannot even begin to understand. One thing we know for sure is that He always wants the best for us, His children, therefore every lesson reaches us when He permits it to, because nothing ever happens without him allowing it to happen - He occupies the Most Highest position ever to exist, therefore all things pass through him before reaching his children; this is due to the reason that He does not allow the devil to tempt us beyond our strengths.

From my personal experience

I always thought that God blessed me with the gift of His divine knowledge but limited the means and the opportunities for me to apply my gift of knowledge, but then I learnt that I had to go through many difficult, dark and life-threatening experiences that will shape my mind to learn about the reality of life so that I can be the person I am growing to be, that is - The person which he desires me to be.

So beloved, Trust Him when He communicates. Keep your faith in Him Growing, No matter how much the devil and his allies attack you [Because the moment you grow in God, Satan feels threatened and he increases his attacks], never let any situation, anyone, or anything to hinder your faith in God from growing. Doing your best to grow in your faith will show God that you trust and depend on Him, and He will fight your battles for you,  while blessing you with an even greater and a beautiful spiritual growth in Him.
