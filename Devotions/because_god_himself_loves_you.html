💖 Because God Himself Loves You

A short devotion on trust, prayer, and the Father-heart of God

God Reveals His Character as Love

Steps to Christ, page 10 says: “The word of God reveals His character. He Himself has declared His infinite love and pity.” When Moses prayed, “Show me Thy glory,” the Lord answered by showing His goodness (Exodus 33:18-19) and proclaiming that He is merciful, gracious, and forgiving (Exodus 34:6-7).

“The Lord, The Lord God, merciful and gracious, long-suffering, and abundant in goodness and truth, keeping mercy for thousands, forgiving iniquity and transgression and sin.” — Exodus 34:6-7

Our Father in Heaven

Jesus taught His disciples to pray, “Our Father which art in heaven…” (Luke 11:2). This is not distant language. It is family language. God is not cold toward His children; He desires their good, growth, safety, and eternal life.

“He is slow to anger, and of great kindness… He delighteth in mercy.” Jonah 4:2; Micah 7:18

Why You Can Trust Him

  • You were made in God’s image
  • Humanity was formed with personal care by His own hands
  • His Word consistently reveals mercy, patience, and compassion
  • Jesus teaches us to draw near to God as children to a loving Father

Daily Application

Lose doubt and fear, and learn to trust Him more each day. Make this your daily reminder: God Himself loves you. No matter how pressed or tried you may be, keep praying and keep learning to trust Him.

Prayer Thought

“Father in heaven, thank You for loving me with mercy and truth. Teach me to trust You more, to pray always, and to walk by faith. In Jesus’ name, Amen.”
