One day, Jesus told the people: "God's Kingdom is like a mustard seed."

The mustard seed is one of the smallest seeds in the garden. But when it is planted in the soil, something amazing happens.

Slowly, it begins to grow. And it keeps on growing, until it becomes a large plant with wide branches. Birds come and rest in its shade, and it becomes a place of life and shelter.

"The kingdom of heaven is like a mustard seed, which a man took and sowed in his field; and this is smaller than all other seeds, but when it is full grown, it is larger than the garden plants and becomes a tree, so that the birds of the air come and nest in its branches." ‚Äî Matthew 13:31-32

This story shows that even the smallest beginnings can grow into something great. A little bit of faith, a kind word, or a small act of love can grow into something powerful in God's Kingdom.

üåü Reflection

  • Start small: Don't underestimate the power of small beginnings. Every mighty thing started with one small step.

  • Become shelter for others: As your faith grows, it becomes a refuge and blessing for those around you.
  • God's kingdom works differently: The world measures by size and strength, but God's kingdom multiplies through faith and love.
