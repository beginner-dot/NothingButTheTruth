Account Options Explained

Here are your 3 choices. This is designed to be simple at first glance, with privacy and freedom respected. All 3 options are free.

Option 1 · Limited · Free

No Sign In

  • Browse immediately without creating an account
  • Best for quick reading/listening
  • Some interactive/personal features are limited
Option 2 · Unlimited · Free

Public Account

  • Unlimited features
  • Your profile is public
  • You can ask and answer questions
  • You can suggest premium options and improvements
Option 3 · Unlimited · Free

Anonymous Account

  • Unlimited features
  • Private anonymous profile
  • No public identity required
  • Best for privacy-first users

Summary: if you want full features, choose Option 2 or Option 3. Option 2 is public; Option 3 is private. Both are free.
