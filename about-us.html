About Us

NothingButTheTRUTH is inspired by the great Advent Movement and centered on the blessed hope of the Second Advent of Jesus Christ. This website outlines the history, importance, and relevance of the Holy Law of Love — namely the Ten Commandments — throughout history and in our modern world.

Advent Movement Inspired Second Advent Hope Law of Love Scripture First

Our Mission

To present the Word of God clearly for all ages, calling people to love, obedience, and readiness for Christ’s soon return.

“If ye love me, keep my commandments.” John 14:15
“Fear God, and keep his commandments: for this is the whole duty of man.” Ecclesiastes 12:13

What We Offer

  • Bible studies on prophecy, doctrine, and Christian history
  • Devotions and videos focused on practical godly living
  • Ten Commandments teaching for modern life and character formation
  • Interactive resources that make Scripture understandable and memorable

Biblical Foundation

We do not remove or replace Scripture. We stand on it:

“All Scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness.” 2 Timothy 3:16

God, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit

These verses show the unity and working relationship within the Godhead:

“Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God is one LORD.” Deuteronomy 6:4 (God)
“I and my Father are one.” John 10:30 (Jesus)
“The Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things.” John 14:26 (Holy Spirit)
“...baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.” Matthew 28:19 (Their united work)
