Bible Connections

Find the 4 groups of 4 hidden among 16 biblical words. Deduction, knowledge, and strategy required!

How to Play

  • Select 4 words you think belong to the same biblical category
  • Press Submit to check your guess
  • Find all 4 groups to win — but you only get 4 mistakes
  • Groups are color-coded by difficulty: 🟡 → 🟢 → 🔵 → 🟣 (hardest)
  • Some words may seem to fit multiple groups — think carefully!
