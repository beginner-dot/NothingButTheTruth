Bible Memory Match

Flip cards and find matching biblical pairs. Train your memory with prophets, miracles, and Scripture!

How to Play

  • Click a card to flip it and reveal what's underneath
  • Click a second card — if the two cards form a biblical pair, they stay face-up
  • If they don't match, both flip back face-down
  • Remember card positions to find all pairs in fewer moves!
  • Complete the board as fast as possible for the best score
