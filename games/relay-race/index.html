Bible Relay Race Game

How to Play

  1. Team Red starts the game.
  2. Click the Start Game button to begin.
  3. Each team takes turns answering questions.
  4. Questions are timed. You get 30 seconds per question.
  5. If you need help, use the Hint button. But using it reduces your score by half for that question.
  6. Click Submit to check your answer.
  7. If time runs out and no answer was submitted, click Next Question to continue.
  8. Different types of questions will appear — typing, true/false, matching, etc.
