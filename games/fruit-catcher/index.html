Fruit of the Spirit Catcher

Catch the fruits of the Spirit falling from heaven — dodge the works of the flesh!

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control." — Galatians 5:22-23

How to Play

  • Move the basket left and right to catch falling items
  • Catch fruits of the Spirit (🍇🍎🍊🍋🫒🍑🌸🍈💐) for points
  • Avoid the works of the flesh (💀🔥😡🐍☠️) — they cost a life!
  • Golden stars (⭐) give bonus points and a temporary speed boost
  • You have 3 lives. Speed increases as your score grows!
  • Each fruit caught teaches you a verse about that virtue
