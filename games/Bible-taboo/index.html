Bible Gridlock: The Forbidden Phrase - Multiplayer

How to Play

  • Split into teams of 3–6 people.
  • One player is the Describer.
  • The Describer gets a Bible topic & 5 forbidden words.
  • They have 1 minute to describe the topic WITHOUT saying any forbidden word.
  • Their team must guess the topic correctly to earn points.
  • If a forbidden word is said or time runs out, no points are earned and the game moves on only when "Next Question" is pressed.
  • The game consists of 10 questions per play.
  • Host creates a game, shares the session ID. Players join with the code.
  • Game starts only when host presses "Start Game".
