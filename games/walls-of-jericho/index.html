Walls of Jericho

March around the city, sound the trumpets at the right moment, and bring the mighty walls crashing down!

"So the people shouted when the priests blew with the trumpets... the wall fell down flat." — Joshua 6:20

How to Play

  • You'll march around Jericho for 7 laps — just like Joshua's army
  • Trumpet notes will scroll across the hit zone
  • Tap the TRUMPET button when a note enters the gold target zone
  • Perfect timing = maximum wall damage. Early/late = less damage
  • On lap 7: SHOUT! Mash the shout button to bring down the walls!
  • Miss too many notes and the walls stay strong!
