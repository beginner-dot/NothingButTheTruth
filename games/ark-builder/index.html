Ark Builder

Load Noah's Ark with animals and supplies before the flood waters rise!

"Make yourself an ark of gopher wood … and I will establish my covenant with you, and you shall come into the ark." — Genesis 6:14,18

How to Play

  • Items fall from the sky — tap or click to drop them onto the ark
  • Use arrow keys or drag to position items left/right before dropping
  • Stack animals (🦁🐘🦒🐑🕊️) and supplies (🪵🥖🫗) on the ark
  • The flood waters rise steadily — load as many as you can!
  • Missed items fall in the water and are lost. Each pair of animals = bonus!
  • The game ends when the water reaches the ark deck
